We Love Teachers (Literally)

My name is Trent O. I started building MyAttendanceTracker because my lovely wife, Jen O, asked me to please help her track her student attendance more easily than the ways that were available to her. So I built her a simple and easy way to track, view and report on the attendance of all of her classes online.

When I had a system together that she loved, other teachers kept asking her how they could use it too and MyAttendanceTracker was born! I continue to improve and update the software every day, listening to the feeedback of over 50,000 users and especially one user in particular. Thanks, Jen O! Trent O - Husband, Daddy, Founder MyAttendanceTracker
